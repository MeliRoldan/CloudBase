Experience the Thrill of Paragliding

Welcome to Cloudbase Paragliding, your premier destination for tandem paragliding in the breathtaking landscapes of Georgia. With over seven years of experience, we are proud to offer an exhilarating and safe paragliding experience in the stunning mountains of Gudauri.

Why CloudBase?

At Cloudbase Paragliding, safety and satisfaction are our top priorities. Our team of highly skilled pilots is fully licensed, insured, and has extensive experience in tandem paragliding. We are dedicated to providing you with a memorable and secure flight experience, ensuring that you can fully enjoy the majestic views of the mountains.

More Than Just a Flight

Join us for an unforgettable adventure. Whether you’re a thrill-seeker looking for your next adrenaline rush or someone who wants to experience the serenity of flight, Cloudbase Paragliding promises an experience like no other.

Reach for the Sky

Discover the beauty of Gudauri from a whole new perspective with Cloudbase Paragliding. Let us take you to new heights!

Why Gudauri?

Gudauri is a paragliding paradise, offering incredible flying opportunities in every season, even amidst snowy mountains. The ever-changing weather conditions, influenced by the mountainous terrain, add an extra element of excitement and unpredictability to each flight. Our experienced pilots are well-versed in navigating these conditions, ensuring a safe and thrilling adventure every time.

Safety

At Cloudbase Paragliding, your safety is our top priority. Our safety measures and equipment we use to ensure your flight is both thrilling and safe include: Safety Mesures: Experienced Pilots; Weather Monitoring; Pre-Flight Briefing; Regular Equipment Inspections. Paragliding Equipment: Paraglider Wing; Harness; Helmet; Reserve Parachute; Communication Devices; Protective Clothing.

Our Pilots

We take immense pride in our team of highly skilled and dedicated pilots. Each pilot brings a wealth of experience, extensive training, and a passion for paragliding, ensuring you have a safe and unforgettable flight experience.

When you fly with Cloudbase Paragliding, you are flying with a team of pilots who are,licensed, and safety-conscious.

Solo & X-country Paragliding

Our pilots are not only highly skilled but also dedicated to ongoing training, including cross-country paragliding in solo flights. Our continuous improvement ensures they bring the highest level of expertise and safety to every flight.

Inclusivity & Accessibility

At Cloudbase Paragliding, we are dedicated to inclusivity and accessibility. We are proud to accommodate customers with disabilities, including those who use wheelchairs. Our licensed, experienced, and insured pilots are skilled in providing a safe and exhilarating flying experience for everyone, ensuring that each flight is as enjoyable and memorable as possible.
