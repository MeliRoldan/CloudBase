Founded in 2018, Cloudbase Paragliding has grown into one of the leading paragliding companies in Georgia, offering unforgettable experiences in the skies. Nestled in the stunning landscape of Gudauri, our team of professional pilots brings together years of passion, skill, and expertise to ensure you have the adventure of a lifetime.
Our pilots aren’t just experienced—they’re also licensed, insured, and proud members of the Georgian Paragliding Federation. With years of solo and tandem flying experience, including cross-country and competitive events, we prioritize both your safety and enjoyment.
At Cloudbase Paragliding, we believe in inclusivity and giving back to the community. We’ve proudly participated in charitable events and initiatives, including organizing paragliding experiences for individuals with disabilities. These special moments inspire us and affirm our commitment to making the skies accessible for everyone.
We’re also always eager to explore new paragliding locations across Georgia, discovering new spots that highlight the country’s breathtaking natural beauty. For those looking to take their paragliding experience to the next level, many of our pilots are certified instructors who offer lessons for beginners. Our instructors are dedicated to sharing their knowledge and passion, guiding you through your first steps in the world of paragliding.
